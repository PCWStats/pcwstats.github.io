About HEAT Labs

HEAT Labs is a community-driven project aimed at providing World of Tanks: HEAT players with comprehensive and reliable tank statistics and gameplay information. Our mission is to help players make informed decisions about which tanks to research and how to play them effectively.

Created by SINEWAVE and passionate players of World of Tanks: HEAT, this platform aggregates data and presents it in an easy-to-understand format, allowing both new and experienced players to enhance their gameplay experience.

We're constantly working to improve and expand our database with the most accurate and up-to-date information. As this is a beta version, we welcome feedback and suggestions from the community.
